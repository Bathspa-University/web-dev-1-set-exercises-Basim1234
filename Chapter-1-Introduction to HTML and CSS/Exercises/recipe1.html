Chicken Biryani Recipe

The aromatic Asian meal known as Chicken Biryani is made up of fragnant basmati rice, different type of spices and caramelized onions topped with marinated chicken. Every bite gives you a perfect taste of Succulent chicken and fluffy rice. Food enthusiasts worldwide are captivated by the exquisite taste and ageless allure of chicken biryani, which serves as a tribute to the rich culinary legacy of South Asia.

Ingredients:

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken pieces (boneless or with bone)
  • Youghurt
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Onions
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Chilies
  • Food colour
  • Cooking oil
  • Water
  • Salt

Method:

  1. Marinate the Chicken, In a bowl take the chicken pieces add youghurt, minced garlic and ginger and then add green Chilies if needed, add salt, and biryani spices like (coriander, cinnamon, cumin, garam masala etc.) let it marinate for atleast 30 to 45 minutes in refrigerator for good flavours.

  2. Rinse the rice thoroughly until water gets clear, Then soak the rice for atleast 30 minutes this allows the rice to cook evenly. Take a large pot of boil water and add salt into it. Add the soaked rice into the boil water cook until it is 70% ready, Then drain the rice.

  3. Caramelize the onions, first slice the onions and fry them until it gets crispy and brown in colour then put it aside so they can dry.

  4. Layer all the ingredients in a big pot, first add the marinated chicken at the bottom followed by a layer of basmati rice but not whole rice only half amount of rice. Add some caramelized onions, chopped mint and coriander on top. Repeat the layers once again, and ending with the rice on top. Add turmeric water over the rice for colour.

  5. Cooking the biryani now, cover the pot tightly with a lid or wrap it in aluminum foil. Cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes this allows the flavours to meld together and the chicken to cook through.

  6. Serve the biryani now, When its cooked use a fork to gently fluff the biryani, carefully not to break the rice grains.
